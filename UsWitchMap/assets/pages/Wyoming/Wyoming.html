Polly Bartlett

Polly Bartlett is part of the local lore in Wyoming, although it is not clear whether or not the story is legitimate or just an urban legend. Polly Bartlett is said to have been an innkeeper who worked with her father to kill 22 travelers. Known as 'Wyoming's Amazing Poisoner', she would poison her guests and bury them. If the stories are true, she would be Wyoming's most deadly serial killer.[1]
