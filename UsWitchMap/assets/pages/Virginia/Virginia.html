Gadsby's Tavern Museun

Gadsby's Tavern Inn and St. Paul's Cemetary in Virginia are both said to be haunted by a young woman. The origin comes from an 1816 story of a young woman who travelled with her husband to Alexandria, Virginia. On the journey there, she caught a disease and went to Gadsby's Tavern to rest. However, she ended up dying there and told everyone that she wished to remain anonymous. When she was buried, her gravestone was simply marked "Female Stranger". [1]
