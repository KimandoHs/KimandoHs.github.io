Pythian Castle

Pythian Castle is a castle built in Springfield, Missouri by the Knights of Pythias. It was repurposed in World War II by the United States military as a "rehabilitation and entertainment center". Nonetheless, it is believed that the castle is haunted, with ghost stories of torture and suicide.[1]
