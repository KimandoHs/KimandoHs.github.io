The Ghost Hound of Goshen

In Goshen Hill in South Carolina, there are rumors of a dog's spirit that haunts a road. Called the Ghost Hound of Goshen, or "Happy Dog", he is anything but nice. It is said that he "holds a grudge against the townsfolk who killed his innocent master". [1]
