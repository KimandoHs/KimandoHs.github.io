Lums Pond State Park

At Lums Pond State Park in Delaware in the 1870's, a "runaway girl was taken into the woods and killed here", with the murderer never being caught. Ever since then, there have been occasional reports of a 'pleading high pitched voice and muffled scream' coming from Lums Pond. [1]
