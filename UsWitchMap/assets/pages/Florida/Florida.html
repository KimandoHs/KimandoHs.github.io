Florida

Florida has an extensive history of folklore and supposed witchcraft, the most infamous example being the Salem Witch Trials from 1692 to 1693, where at least 25 people died and many more were imprisoned[1]. One of the 13 colonies founded by the British, the history of witchcraft in Florida extends back to the 17th century.

Skunk Ape

The Skunk Ape is a creature associated with the state of Florida. Skunk Ape is often called Florida's version of Big Foot. The Skunk Ape is described as having "generally black or 'dark' long hair or fur".[1]. Its height ranges from 4 feet tall, to potentially 10, with 7.45 being the average. This is quite similar to the average of Big Foot, who reportedly has an estimated height of 7.57 feet. The Skunk Ape is commonly said to have a bad odor, being described as "smelly".
