Windsor Hotel

The Windsor Hotel was opened in Georgia in 1892. The Windsor Hotel is popular enough to have hosted notable historical figures, such as Al Capone and Jimmy Carter. However, this hotel has had reports of being haunted. It is said that the ghost of a little girl haunts the hotel after being killed by being pushed down an elevator shaft. [1]
