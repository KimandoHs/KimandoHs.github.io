Haunted Buildings in Anacortes

Abandoned buildings in Anacortes, a city in Washington state, have been thought to be haunted, though many of them have been demolished. The Anacortes Hotel at 8th street and J avenue was one of these supposedly haunted buildings. Other buildings suspected of being haunted include "The Opera House on 6th Street" and "the Townsend Hall, located at 12th and O Avenue". [1].
