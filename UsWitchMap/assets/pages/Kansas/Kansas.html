Albino Woman

One of Topeka's most famous lore is that of the albino woman who was known to walk around her neighborhood and stare at children. When she died, people began to report sightings of a "glowing white female figure". These sightings were commonly around the location she was buried, in Rochester Cemetary. There have even been claims of this apparition staring through windows to watch children sleep. Nonetheless, it was never known to hurt anybody. [1]
