New Hope

New Hope is a borough in Pennsylvania that has been described as a ghost-filled town, and one that 'has attracted members of the supernatural'. One of the many places said to be haunted in New Hope is The Logan Inn, which has been reported to harbor several spirits. The Logan Inn is so infamous that it has been called "one of the most genuinely haunted structures in the nation". [1].
