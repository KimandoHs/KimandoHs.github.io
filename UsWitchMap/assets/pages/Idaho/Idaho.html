Ghosts of ISU

Frazier Hall

It has been said that Frazier Hall at ISU is haunted by an entity named Alex. Alex is thought to be a benevolent ghost, presumed to be the ghost of a child of a professor. Alex is believed to have enjoyed coming to Frazier hall so much, that he continued to come back after death to play tricks on the living. [1]

Graveley Hall

Graveley has been said to have "a strong presense". Supernatural occurrences such as random footsteps, thumping noises, and locked windows opening for no reason have been reported here. [1]
