Room 315

The Alaskan Hotel in Juneau is known for supposedly being haunted, with its most haunted room being room 315. Although there have never been any sightings of ghosts in that room, the owner of the hotel, Bettye Adams, has said that she "feels things". Adams claims that the hotel has hauntings all throughout the bar, basement, second floor, and more. The rumors gained even more notoriety when a navy veteran asked for room 315, and ended up jumping out the window for unknown reasons. [1]
