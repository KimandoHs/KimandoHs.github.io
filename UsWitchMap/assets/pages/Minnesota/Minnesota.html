The Boy in the Red Cap

"The Boy in the Red Cap" is the name of a ghost commonly seen at St. Olaf campus. It has been speculated that he is the ghost of a man who died in the 1980s. Supposely, he got caught in a tunnel cave-in nearby campus. [1].
