Route 2A

Route 2A is known in Maine for being both haunted and dangerous. In addition to claims that many truckers have died on this route while bringing potatoes to Boston, there have also been claims of sightings of a woman screaming about her husband being trapped in their vehicle. When drivers get out of their car to help her, the woman vanishes.

Others haved claimed that they have seen a little girl on the side of the road, who is rumored to have been killed after being hit by a semi-truck on this route. [1]
