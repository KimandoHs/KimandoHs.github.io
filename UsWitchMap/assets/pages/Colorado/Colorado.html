336 Main Street

336 Main Street in Colorado completed construction in 1928. For the first few decades of its existence, it was a furniture company where nothing out of the ordinary happened. Recently, it has been a popular location for restaurants. Previous restaurant owners at this location have reported strange phenomena occuring. They have claimed to hear footsteps and voices when the restaurant was supposed to be empty. They also claimed to see ghostly figures near the women's bathroom. [1]
