Little Girl in the Cinema

Tennessee Tech, a university in Tennessee, is said to have numerous hauntings. Perhaps one of the more famous stories is that of the little girl in the cinema. The Varsity Building currently on campus used to be the Varsity Cinema Theatre. This theatre operated for 30 years, where its employees are said to have experienced many strange phenomena. A little girl's laughter is said to have been heard "almost every night around closing". The sound of this laughter would usually happen inside the projector room. It happened with such frequency that most employees would refuse to go in there by themselves. [1]
