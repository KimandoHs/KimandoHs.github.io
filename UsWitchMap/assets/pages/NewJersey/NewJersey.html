The Devil's Tree

In New Jersey, there exists a tree called the Devil's Tree., which is said to be one of the most haunted places in the state. The lore behind it is that a farmer commited family annihilation before hanging himself on the tree. Since then, there have been numerous reported suicides and murders revolving around this tree. Those who try to cut down the tree "are said to be cursed". [1]
