McRaven House

The McRaven house in Mississippi is often called the "most haunted house in Mississippi". It is said that the house is haunted by numerous spirits. Known history of the house extends all the way back to 1836, when Mary Elizabeth Howard died while giving birth in the upstairs bedroom. Her spirit is supposedly the "most active" of the bunch. Since her death, at least four other people are said to have died inside of this house. [1]
