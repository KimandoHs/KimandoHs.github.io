Albany Rural Cemetary

In the state of New York, Albany Rural Cemetary has commonly been proclaimed a site of hauntings and ghost stories. People have alleged seeing strange things, such as glowing orbs, or floating pajamas. In 1869, a "mysterious ghostly figure wandering around Consecration Lake" was reported by a Troy newspaper. Decades later, another urban legend, which mentioned sounds of a "phantom horse galloping down the cemetary roads" being heard from the cemetary, came about after a horse was killed after colliding with a monument there.
