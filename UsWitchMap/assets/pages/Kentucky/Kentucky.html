Florence Schneider Hall

Florence Schneider Hall is a building at Western Kentucky University. Urban legend has it that during spring break many decades ago, a man climbed through the window of this dorm with an axe and murdered a girl who was sleeping there. The girl is said to have scratched the door of another girl's room before dying. Since then, the girl has been haunting the hallways of the dorm, scratching on doors. Students have reported hearing "door slammings, footsteps". [1]
