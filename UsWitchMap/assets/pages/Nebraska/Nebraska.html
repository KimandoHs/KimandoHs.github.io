Neihardt Residence Hall

Neihardt Residential Hall, locaated at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, was used "as an infirmary in the 1930s and 40s for polio outbreaks". Ever since then, Neihardt Resident Hall has been reported to be haunted by spirits. People have claimed to have seen the ghosts of young women here. [1]
