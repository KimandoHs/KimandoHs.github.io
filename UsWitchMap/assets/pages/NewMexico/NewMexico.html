La Residencia

La Residencia was the name of an old hospital in Santa Fe. La Residencia was said to be haunted. It was said that the basement of the building would have "blood oozing from the walls". There was also a infamous story associated with room 311, where workers would claim to hear a continuous crying sound coming from it late at night. One nurse remarked that it sounded more like a child gasping for breath than a crying sound. [1]
