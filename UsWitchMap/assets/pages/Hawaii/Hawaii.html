Punahou

Punahou is a private school in Honolulu, Hawaii.

The Shadow of Dillingham Hall

Travis Flazer, the technical director at Punahou, claims to have experienced paranormal activity at Dillingham hall. He claims that on the way back to his office upstairs, he saw a shadow on the wall that resembled a "blurry, slender figure". The figure disappeared, but as he walked by the wall it was on, he felt its presence yet again. Feeling too terrifed to turn around, he made his way back to his office where he felt safe from this shadow. [1]
