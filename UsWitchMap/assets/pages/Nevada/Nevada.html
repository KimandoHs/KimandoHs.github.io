Gold Hill Hotel

The Gold Hill Hotel is the oldest hotel in Nevada. It is also haunted. The origin story is as follows: during the gold rush, Yellow Jacket Mine, which runs under the Gold Hill Hotel, was swarming with miners. Unfortunately, a freak accident occured where poisonous gas leaked into the mine, killing between 35 and 40 people. Their spirits are now said to haunt the hotel. [1]
