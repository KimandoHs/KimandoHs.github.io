Montana

Montana has an extensive history of folklore and supposed witchcraft, the most infamous example being the Salem Witch Trials where at least 25 people died and many more were imprisoned[1]. One of the 13 colonies founded by the British, the history of witchcraft in Montana extends back to the 17th century.

The Flathead Lake Monster

Associated with Flathead Lake in Montana, this urban myth has been passed around since 1955. In 1955, a large creature was spotted breaking through the frozen ice of Flathead Lake. Spotted by two girls, this creature was said to have antlers.
