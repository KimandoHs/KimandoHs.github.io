Patty

The Kappa Delta sorority at Utah State University claims to house a benevolent ghost. Named Patty, no one is quite sure where she came from. It is said that every year, the girl staying in room 13 has had a dream of a "girl in a white dress trying to enter the house". Patty supposedly 'likes to mess with people', flickering lights whenever somebody is acting bratty. In addition to that, Patty likes to check in on the girls as well, opening doors for them late at night. [1]
