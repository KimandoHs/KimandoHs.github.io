Doherty Hotel

The Doherty Hotel has been open and ran by the Doherty family since the 1920s. In 1938, a former purple gang attorney, Isaiah Leebove, was murdered in the bar of the hotel by his cousin Jack Livingston. Since then, there have been rumors of the hotel's haunting. More specifically, there have been reports of the family matriarch, Helen Doherty, being heard in a back room. [1].
