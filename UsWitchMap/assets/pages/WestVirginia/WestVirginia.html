Haymond House

In Braxton county, West Virginia, there exists a house called the 'Haymond House' which people say is haunted. Since the December of 2019, the owners of the house had begun experiencing unexplainable phenomena. This includes things such as a woman in white which was "seen by several observers", and a tall slim grey figure being seen throughout the house. Despite this, the owners of the house claim that they are benevolent ghosts and that guests are welcome. [1]
